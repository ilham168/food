About Us

Visi

To be a trusted and inspiring source of information for culinary lovers around the world, by providing authentic recipes and culinary history from various parts of the world.

Misi

Educating about Culinary History: Providing in-depth information about the history and origins of various dishes from various countries, so that readers not only learn how to cook, but also understand the culture and traditions behind each dish.

Inspiring Culinary Exploration: Encourages readers to explore and try different cuisines from around the world, opening up new insights into the diversity of flavors and cooking techniques.
